Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12/07/2024

  1. Introduction

    Welcome to Alchemy Aleworks. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit our website and interact with our services.

  2. Data We Collect

    We collect the following types of personal data from users:

    • Contact Information: Name, email address
    • Account Information: Email address, password
    • Order Information: Name, billing address, shipping address, payment details
  3. How We Collect Your Data

    We collect personal data through:

    • Forms: When you create an account, place an order, or subscribe to a newsletter
    • User Accounts: Information provided during registration and account management
  4. Purpose of Data Collection

    We collect personal data for the following purposes:

    • Account Creation: During account sign-up, users need to provide an email address and password
    • Order Processing: To process and fulfil orders, including shipping and billing
  5. Legal Requirements

    There are no specific legal requirements for collecting the types of data we collect beyond standard business practices for processing orders and maintaining accounts.

  6. Data Sharing

    We do not share users' data with third parties except internally for providing assistance with payment and shipping.

    We do not sell users' personal data to third parties.

  7. User Rights

    Users have the following rights regarding their personal data:

    • Access and Correction: Users can amend their saved delivery information from their profile.
    • Deletion: Users can contact support if they would like to delete their account.
    Opting Out:
    • Users cannot opt out of data collection or data sharing as the data is required to deliver products and services.
  8. Data Security

    We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect users' data, including:

    • Encryption: Data is encrypted during transmission.
    • Secure Servers: Data is stored on secure servers with access controls.
    Data Retention:
    • We retain users' data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
  9. Cookies

    We use the following types of cookies on our website:

    • Functional Cookies: Necessary for website functionality, including an age verification cookie.
    Managing Cookie Preferences:
    • The age verification cookie is essential for complying with legal requirements to restrict access to age-sensitive content. As such, users cannot opt out of this cookie. However, users can manage other cookie preferences through their browser settings.
  10. Third-Party Services

    We do not use any third-party services such as Google Analytics or social media plugins on our website.

  11. Policy Updates

    We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website.

  12. Contact Information

    If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at:

    Alchemy Aleworks

    Email: support@alchemyaleworks.com
