Orders and Returns

Last Updated: 12/07/2024

Order Policy:

  1. Order Placement:
    • Orders can be placed through our website alchemyaleworks.com or by contacting customer support at support@alchemyaleworks.com.
    • By placing an order, you agree to our Terms and Conditions and acknowledge that you are legally capable of entering into a binding contract.
  2. Order Confirmation:
    • Upon placing an order, you will receive an email confirmation with details of your order.
    • Please review the confirmation and notify us immediately of any discrepancies.
  3. Payment:
    • Payment must be made in full at the time of placing your order.
    • We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express (Amex), Discover, Diners Club, JCB, and UnionPay.
  4. Order Cancellation:
    • You may cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it, provided it has not yet been shipped or processed.
    • To cancel, please contact us immediately at suppport@alchemyaleworks.com.
  5. Out of Stock Items:
    • In the event that an item you ordered is out of stock, we will notify you promptly and provide options for a replacement or refund.

Returns and Refunds Policy:

  1. Returns Eligibility:
    • We accept returns on merchendise items within 7 days of delivery, provided the items are unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.
  2. Return Process:
    • To initiate a return, please contact us at support@alchemyaleworks.com to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
    • Pack the item securely and include the RMA number on the package.
    • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, except in cases of damaged or faulty items.
  3. Refunds:
    • Refunds will be processed within 5 days of receiving the returned item.
    • Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment used during the purchase.
  4. Damaged or Faulty Items:
    • If you receive a damaged or faulty item, please contact us immediately with details and photographic evidence.
    • We will arrange for a replacement or refund as soon as possible.
  5. Exclusions:
    • Certain items may not be eligible for returns due to hygiene reasons or if they have been personalised or customised.
